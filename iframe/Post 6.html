Cluster

The are a few things to state about this one in particular. First and foremost, the image is supposed to feel cold, serious, and dead. The inspiration for this project were old black and white photographs, and what feeling they cause. Most of these photographs create much of this same atmosphere of seeming heartless and broken nowadays, although not necessarily goal of the photo. This was mostly prevelant, at least to my experience, in photos from around the 60s. Very frozen in time, more than a photo usually is, and almost like it is about to awaken and move again.

There are two other reasons for this project's existence, though. One is the visualisation of a place, the home of creatures, simply called »Binaries« - A concept explained in the post that comes before this one. But in simple terms, a ficticious group of creatures.
The second reason is as a photo-like background of an EP (Extended Play). It is meant as a promotional image, as well as the main title image, and serves to sell the feeling the tracks seek to express.
