Lost Soul

This project is a special one. Essentially, it is a design for a hoodie that does not exist - not yet, but ever. But what is the goal in making the desgin for something, and then never choosing to realize it? Simply a gag of sorts. Not every product has to be a thing, and not every idea needs to be in the real world. The reason some designs don't get turned into actual products is because they are either not meant for it, or they are only there to create interest in a possible brand.

But my main reason for this project's existence goes a bit further. There was a moment where I was sitting in a video call with a few friends of mine where we discussed possible ideas for projects to work on. This was one of those ideas. Because one of them was talking about how they had this very specific idea in their head for a hoodie design they wanted, but that would surely never happen. And so, I didn't want to let their hopes down.
