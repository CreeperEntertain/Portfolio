The Wilderness

Created as the title image for this portfolio specifically, it represents a lot of the design principles I choose. Clean shapes made from artificial materials, usually combined with nature elements like water and plants. Together, this forms a design, usable as either wallpaper, a cover like here, a presentable media asset for a self-marketing gig, or just generally a presentation.

Originally, it was purely meant as an artwork to be shared on social media, but has since transformed into exactly what is mentioned above. Not because of an inability to decide, but rather because the goal shifted.
