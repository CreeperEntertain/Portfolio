Foshwave's BO22

The title of this project refers to a collaborative music group called »Foshwave« and their »Best of 2022« song collection. An effort to respect the works of the ten artists featured on the statue, and to promote the group to more aspiring producers.

It is not a statue that's claimed by simply one person, but the entire groupd of people mentioned. And that is because of what makes these producers publish under the Foshwave name - a trust founded relationship, in which artists, advertisers, and publishers work together to bring more nieche music to more people, and in the same move promote music of people who would have otherwise usually either had a very small audience, or none at all. For many producers, getting their works out there is a hard endeavor, and seeing this process being simplified by groups like this is a great thing.
