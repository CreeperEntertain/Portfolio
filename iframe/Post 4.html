Baroque

This design is part of a series that puts this persona in different places and times. In this instance, a house whose style is like a prime example for the time. Rich textures, objects, paintings, furniture, and lecture.

But not every thing is exactly like the topic would make it be. There is a strong sense of otherworldly magic or power, represented mostly in the floating rock, and elements that do not fit. For instance, the wallpaper is more modern than those times, there were no aquariums this advanced, and the lighting is much too dim as opposed to the bright appearance of most of the interiors from the time.
