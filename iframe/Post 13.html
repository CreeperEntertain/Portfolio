Logo

Seen here is my logo in a modern design. This project was really just started out as a sort of motion. I was scrolling around Instagram the other day, searching for inspiration, and there was this one post with a very geometric, layered design someone presented. I liked it to the point where I decided to recreate it and put a spin on it.

Another reason I chose to do it this way specifically was because it happened during a period where I began trying out new ways of working in 3D, as well as new tools to add a little spice to my designs - Mainly to make them more interesting and appealing while still keeping them simplistic. And I think this sort of layered design with water does just that. it adds a lot of points to look at while not making the overall shape too confusing or chaotic.
