Patio

Here, you can see a more architechture based design. At any time, the decision could have been made to place a product, or an object in the center to break up the image, or to add contrast, but since you see the version here, that means I decided against it.

Why is that?
The reason I chose against it is because I wanted the environment to be the main point of the project, and not a tool to present a product. Usually very counter-productive in a portfolio like this, but there is a reason for this as well. I simply wanted to present my capabilities in making realistic scenes by themselves. Because any product visualisation needs at least some kind of environment, cluttered or not.
