Dynasty

The main reason this project turned out the way it did was because of the direction it is designed for. Oriented mainly towards a very medieval and glorious style mixed with elements found on movie posters or promotional images, the reason it exists is for a planned album that never finished and released.

The name dynasty was chosen because of the topic of the album. The long fight between the king's families and the church often found in history. A fight of power over the kingdoms, and one over who seemed more glorious, rich, beautiful, and believable in their goals. Dynasty as a word itself then underlines the meaning of those powerful failies themselves. Essentially only a few bloodlines eternally bound together in this everlasting drama.
