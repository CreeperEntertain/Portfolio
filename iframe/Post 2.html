Computing

This design is supposed to represent the principle of an algorithm at its core.
It does not matter if you see a futuristic computer, a city, or a power plant - the principle always stays the same. Because algorithms not only describe processes in a computer, but can be compared to both of these other principles as well.

In simplest terms - a computer functions by signals going from place to place - a city functions by inhabitants going from place to place - a power plant functions by energy going from place to place. It is an endless cycle.
