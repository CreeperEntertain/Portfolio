Binary

This design carries its name because of what it depicts - A Binary.
Binaries are a concept found in the ARG (Alternate Reality Game) they are a part of. They are very clear beings whose goal is to serve a nameless entity whose goal is to take over the multiverse. There is a chant that can be heard around these beings, and it is like a clicking, paired with some synthesizer-like elements sprinkled around.

The reason this design specifically is part of thos portfolio is to represent that not every design has to be part of a front page, or the title to a product, and can surve a purpose as part of an explanatory page, or a (forum) post discussing the topic.
